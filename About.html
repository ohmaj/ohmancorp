About Us

Because Ohman Automation Corp is a small business, we understand the needs and challenges of your small business. We will treat your business as though it were our own. We have a somewhat unique business method in that we will be your "part-time IT administrator". We may not have our own office in your place of business, but you can picture that as being the case. We bill on an as needed basis, minimum 1/10th of an hour (6 minutes). There are automation opportunites within every business. We specialize in analyzing businesses to identify affordable and cost-justified processes that could benefit from a technology plan.
